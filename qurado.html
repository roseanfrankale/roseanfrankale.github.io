01 — Overview

The Challenge

Qurado, an emerging e-commerce platform, needed a complete overhaul of its user interface and checkout process to compete in a crowded market.

The Problem

The existing site had a confusing sitemap and a cumbersome checkout flow, leading to high cart abandonment rates. The branding was inconsistent and failed to build trust with users.

Our Solution

As a UI/UX consultant, I redesigned the entire sitemap, checkout, and payment flows. I also collaborated on creating a modern branding identity with new typography and a cohesive look and feel.

02 — Process

Design Journey

A focused consultancy project to rapidly improve the user experience.

Sitemap & Flow Redesign
