01 — Overview

Elevating an E-Commerce Experience

Qurado is an e-commerce platform that connects consumers directly with the best local food producers throughout Spain. The products are harvested or processed just before shipment, ensuring maximum freshness and quality.

I was brought on in a minor consulting capacity to demonstrate how modern design tools and principles could enhance the user experience and increase website engagement. The goal was to propose improvements to the existing site, focusing on branding, user flow, and overall aesthetics.

Project Highlights

  • Provided UI/UX consulting to improve an existing e-commerce platform.
  • Demonstrated the value of Figma, Adobe XD, and Notion for increasing traffic and engagement.
  • Developed new branding concepts for a more modern and sleek look.
  • Created a new sitemap to improve user-friendliness and site structure.
  • Designed and tested new checkout and payment workflows to reduce friction.
02 — Design Process

From Concept to Deployment

A consultative approach to improving user experience.

Sitemap & Structure

My first task was to create a sitemap for the new design. I used the existing sitemap as a starting point and made a few changes to the structure to make it more user-friendly and intuitive for customers navigating the site.

Branding & Visuals

We worked on creating a few new branding concepts for the new design. We wanted to create a more modern and sleek look for the app, so we experimented with different color schemes and typography to better reflect the premium quality of the products.

Workflows & Prototypes

We worked on checkout flows and payment flows for the new design. We wanted to make the checkout process as smooth and easy as possible for our users, so we tested a few different designs and made a few changes to the flow to reduce friction.

Deployment & Final Touches

Utilizing the same color palette and typography, we created a modernized design and aesthetic for the site. This included a hero video to introduce the brand and its commitment to quality.
