    About Me

    Hello, I’m Rosean Frank-Alexander! I’m a tech professional with over a decade of experience and a passion for creating user-centered solutions. My experience includes roles in product, design, and software development.
    I specialize in blending technical expertise with strategic insight, creating seamless, user-centered experiences that align with business objectives.

    My journey in technology began when my grandmother surprised me with a desktop filled with CDs—one of them featuring Weezer’s Buddy Holly music video.
    That moment became the foundation for my future interests, sparking a lasting affinity for technology, software, and music.
