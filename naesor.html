Overview

As part of the Google UX Design Certificate, I undertook the challenge of designing a dedicated mobile application for music fans. The concept focuses on "Super Fans" who want deeper engagement with artists beyond simple streaming.

The Problem

Users interested in specific artists struggle to find a centralized place to pre-order albums, save releases, and access exclusive content before the official drop date.

The Goal

Design an app that visually represents the artist's brand while creating an engaging, seamless experience for users to pre-save and purchase music.

I conducted user research to identify the target audience: dedicated music fans who value early access. I created detailed personas to understand their pain points, such as the frustration of missing release dates or navigating cluttered streaming interfaces.
