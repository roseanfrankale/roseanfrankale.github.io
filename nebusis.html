Nebusis®

AI ISO Technologies to Minimize Operational Risk

Role

Product Manager, UI/UX

Timeline

Nov 2020 - June 2023

Tools

Adobe CC, Figma, Elementor

Link

Live Site

Overview

Nebusis® is a simplified Software as a Service (SaaS) solution based on recognized ISO Management system standards. My journey began with correcting translated course materials but quickly evolved into a full-scale digital transformation.

I led the website redesign, branding, and operations management, eventually overseeing the integration of Generative AI to automate ISO policy creation.

The Problem

The original site wasn't designed for mobile. During a critical global client onboarding, the website crashed with no backup, halting operations.

The Solution

We rebuilt the system using saved Figma assets, switched to vector-based A/B testing, and deployed 4 new landing pages using Elementor and AI.

The Process

From Crisis to Deployment

01

V1 & Early Process

I initially started restructuring the site based on foundational design principles from the Google UI/UX Course. The goal was to adjust the existing infrastructure for mobile responsiveness, as the original site was not built with mobile in mind.

02

The Crash & V2 Plan

Mid-way through onboarding 2 of 4 locations for a global client, the website crashed. There was no backup. Thankfully, I had assets saved in Figma and on an external drive. We made the decision to pivot quickly, updating colors and fonts to freshen the brand while rebuilding.

03

Marketing & Design

Due to urgency, we used vectors for A/B testing instead of static images. I simultaneously created marketing materials including GIFs, email templates, and copy. This phase allowed me to explore Neumorphism and improve my mockup skills.

04

Deployment & AI

We utilized Generative AI to create ISO policies dynamically based on client needs. Using WordPress and Elementor, I navigated the limitations of free services to successfully deploy four distinct landing pages and a pricing page.
