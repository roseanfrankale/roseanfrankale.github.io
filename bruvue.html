IoT-Powered Beverage Inventory Management

Role

Director of Customer Success

Timeline

Jan 2019 - July 2019

Stack

Node.js, React, IoT

Social

Facebook

Overview

The rapid growth of craft beer created new inventory and sales tracking challenges for bars, distributors, and breweries, making it difficult to know what consumers were drinking in real-time.

The Problem

Manual inventory tracking was inefficient and prone to error, leading to beer wastage, theft, and a lack of accurate data for sales and production planning.

The Solution

BruVue developed an IoT data inventory system with faucet-mounted sensors to track beer movement in real-time, providing a SaaS dashboard with analytics.
