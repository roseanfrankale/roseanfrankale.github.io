Real-time beverage inventory data for bars, distributors and breweries.

IoT | Product Design | Inspiration

Problem

Due to the rapid growth of craft beer increasing the number of tap handles at bars, the number of SKUs at distributors, and the number of new products that brewers must produce proposed new challenges in the industries three-tier supply chain.
It’s difficult at every tier of the supply chain to know what consumers are drinking, and this problem is becoming more complex as product diversity in the market increases.

Approach

BruVue developed a IoT data inventory system intended to prevent beer wastage. Its features included real-time keg inventory, matching actual pours with charged pours, digital beer menu, analytics dashboard, foaming & waste pattern detection, data stored in cloud, and allowed mobile device access.

Solution

BruVue offered Internet-connected sensors that were dropped on beer faucets and tracked the movement of beer in real-time, enabling breweries to see product inventory and prevented the theft or wastage of beer.
