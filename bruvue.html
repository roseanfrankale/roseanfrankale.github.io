01 — Overview

The Challenge

The rapid growth of craft beer created new inventory and sales tracking challenges for bars, distributors, and breweries, making it difficult to know what consumers were drinking in real-time.

My Role

Director of Customer Success, Product Contributor

Tech Stack

Node.js React IoT

The Problem

Manual inventory tracking was inefficient and prone to error, leading to beer wastage, theft, and a lack of accurate data for sales and production planning across the three-tier supply chain.

Our Solution

BruVue developed an IoT data inventory system with faucet-mounted sensors to track beer movement in real-time, providing a SaaS dashboard with analytics, waste detection, and digital menus.
