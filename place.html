Overview

What is Place?

Place is an AI-powered tour management platform designed specifically for emerging bands and independent musicians. The platform streamlines the complex process of planning, booking, and executing tours by leveraging intelligent automation and data-driven insights.

Unlike traditional booking platforms that require extensive industry connections, Place democratizes tour management by providing smaller artists with professional-grade tools, venue recommendations, routing optimization, and automated logistics—all powered by machine learning.

"Making touring accessible for every artist, no matter how small the stage."

The Goal

Empower independent musicians with enterprise-level tour management capabilities through AI-driven venue matching, intelligent routing algorithms, automated booking workflows, and real-time logistics coordination—removing the traditional barriers that prevent emerging artists from touring professionally.

THE VISION

Tour smarter, not harder

If you're an independent musician trying to book your first tour, you know the overwhelming challenge. Spreadsheets of venues, endless cold emails, routing nightmares, and the constant fear of booking unprofitable shows. What if AI could handle the logistics?

I'm designing Place to solve these problems. An intelligent tour manager that analyzes your genre, fanbase, and goals to recommend optimal venues, generate efficient routes, automate booking communications, and ensure every show makes financial sense for emerging artists.
