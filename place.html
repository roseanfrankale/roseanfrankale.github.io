Overview

What is Place?

Place is an innovative location-based platform designed to connect people with meaningful experiences in their physical environment. The project aims to reimagine how users discover, interact with, and share information about the spaces around them.

Unlike traditional location services that focus on ratings and reviews, Place prioritizes genuine storytelling and community-driven narratives to help users form authentic connections with the places they visit.

"Every place has a story. We're building the platform to tell them."

The Goal

Create an immersive, story-driven platform that allows users to explore locations through curated narratives, community insights, and interactive mapping features that prioritize authentic experiences over commercial recommendations.

THE VISION

Discover places that matter

If you've ever traveled somewhere new and felt overwhelmed by generic recommendations, you know the frustration. Star ratings and sponsored listings don't tell you the real story of a place. What if discovery felt more human?

I'm designing Place to solve these problems. An immersive platform that connects you with locations through authentic stories, local insights, and meaningful community connections.
