I once rocked out alot.

As a professional bass player, I had the privilege of performing with various talented musicians and bands. My responsibilities included maintaining rhythm, composing bass lines, coordinating with drummers for tight grooves, rehearsing with ensembles, recording sessions, equipment maintenance, and touring. These experiences shaped me into the musician I am today.

Toon & The Real Laww a Hip-Hop duo.

Bass Guitarist
2013-2015
