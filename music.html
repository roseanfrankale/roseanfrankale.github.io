Music & Sound

A look back at a decade behind the bass.

As a professional bass player, I had the privilege of performing with various talented musicians and bands. My responsibilities included maintaining rhythm, composing bass lines, coordinating with drummers, rehearsing with ensembles, recording sessions, and touring.

BoxBomb

Bass Guitarist • 2003–2009

This project gave me the foundational skills that transformed into a 10-year career. We released three albums and were signed to Tragic Hero Records, a sub-label of Warner Bros. Records.

Notable Performances:

Thrice, Toadies, The Get Up Kids, American Aquarium, Killa Priest, He is Legend, MuteMath, Will Hoge, Copeland, Underoath, Colossus, House of Fools, Hobex, The Enemy Lovers, Telescreen, Bayside, Veara

Lila

Bass Guitarist • 2011–2016

LiLa's sound can be described as "Hip-Folk," blending the diverse musical backgrounds of its members to mix rap, folk, rock, and even dubstep. We garnered significant local attention, leading to dynamic and energetic performances.

Notable Performances:

Girl Talk, Who's Bad

Toon & The Real Laww

Bass Guitarist • 2013–2015

This was a hip-hop duo that formed a short-term backing band. We performed at various venues and events in the Triangle area, mixing conscious rap with boom-bap beats.

Notable Performances:

Run the Jewels, 2 Live Crew, Meek Mill, Machine Gun Kelly, GZA, Gym Class Heroes, Big Sean, Mike Posner, Rapsody, King Mez, Kooley High

Erik Smallwood

Touring / Session Bassist

Erik is an Alabama-born (Muscle Shoals) Singer-Songwriter, Producer, and Worship Leader that I played with in a cover band and also recorded bass tracks for his albums. No notable performances to list.

Lions at Lunchtime

Bass Guitarist • 2010–2011

Lions at Lunchtime asked me to hop on for a few shows, and I spent a year working with them on several songs to complete their EP. This project was more of a hobby and a relaxed, creative experience. No notable performances to list.
