Hi! I'm Rosean Frank-Alexander

I'm a Product Thinker and Designer with a formal background in Information Technology. My work revolves around creating user experiences that are thoughtful and innovative, often blending emerging technologies with practical design thinking.

My journey across various roles has shaped the way I approach product design—always curious, collaborative, and driven by impact. I focus on connecting users, stakeholders, and business goals through experiences that feel intuitive and intentional.

Creative Foundation

With over a decade as a professional bass guitarist and deep knowledge in audio/visual production, I approach every project with a musician's discipline: finding the rhythm, establishing the structure, and ensuring every component plays together perfectly. I bring creativity, technical precision, and a unique perspective to every team.

My experience touring with bands and recording in studios taught me collaboration, listening, and how to create something greater than the sum of its parts. I bring those same principles into every project and team I work with.

Building Systems & Experiences for 10+ Years

I landed my first technical role over a decade ago, and since then, I’ve been blessed to work with great teams across fintech, healthcare, IoT, and cloud services, moving from the precision of PCI-DSS compliance and ISO auditing to the creative execution of UI/UX and Frontend Engineering.

Creating and Breaking Things

As a child, I learned a considerable amount about how things work by just taking things apart and attempting to rebuild them.
